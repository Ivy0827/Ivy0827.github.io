    • BADGING SYSTEM

    Instead of grades, we using a badging system.

    Each student receives a skill badge from the panel of judges if they have satisfied the challenge or problem effectively.

    However, in addition to that if you have excelled at the challenge, you will receive “ribbons” Gold, Silver or Bronze from the panel.

    These are digital merits that students can add to their industry-ready portfolios.
